# Privacy Policy - Exoda

**Effective Date:** November 25, 2024  
**Last Updated:** November 25, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Exoda ("we," "us," "our," or the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Financial Data:** When you use Exoda, you voluntarily input financial information including transactions, amounts, dates, payment methods, entity names, and expense categories. This information is entirely optional and provided at your discretion.

**Notes and Descriptions:** Any notes, descriptions, or additional information you add to transactions.

**User Preferences:** Language preferences, currency settings, and other customization options you select in the App's settings.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:** We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

**Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, actions taken, and time spent in the App.

**Crash Reports:** If the App crashes, we may collect technical information to help us improve stability and performance.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Exoda uses the information you provide for the following purposes:

- **Providing Services:** To deliver the App's core functionality, including transaction tracking, categorization, reporting, and data analysis.
- **App Improvement:** To understand how users interact with the App and to identify areas for improvement.
- **Technical Support:** To diagnose technical issues and provide customer support.
- **Security:** To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security issues, and technical problems.
- **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

**Privacy-First Approach:** Exoda stores all your financial data locally on your device using SQLite database technology. We do not transmit, upload, or store your personal financial information on our servers or cloud services.

**Your Control:** You have complete control over your data. It remains on your device and is not shared with third parties unless you explicitly export it.

### 4.2 Data Security

While we implement security measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and any backup files you create.

### 4.3 Backup and Export

The App provides features to export your database for backup purposes. You are solely responsible for:
- Storing backup files securely
- Protecting backup files with appropriate access controls
- Managing the lifecycle of backup files

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 No Third-Party Sharing

Exoda does not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial data to third parties. We do not share your data with marketing partners, advertisers, or any external organizations.

### 5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental request. We will attempt to provide you with notice of such requests unless prohibited by law.

### 5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 Third-Party Libraries

Exoda may use third-party libraries and services to provide functionality. These may include:

- **Flutter SDK:** For app development and runtime
- **SQLite:** For local database storage

## 7. Children's Privacy

Exoda is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

- **Right to Access:** You can request access to your personal information
- **Right to Correction:** You can request correction of inaccurate information
- **Right to Deletion:** You can request deletion of your information
- **Right to Data Portability:** You can request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 11.

## 9. Data Retention

**Local Data:** Financial data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete it through the App's settings or uninstall the App.

**Backup Files:** Backup files you export are retained for as long as you maintain them. We recommend regularly reviewing and securely deleting old backup files.

**Server Data:** We do not retain your financial data on our servers. Any technical logs or crash reports are retained for a limited period (typically 30 days) for troubleshooting purposes.
